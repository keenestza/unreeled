Patty Granville Stars in Hello Dolly! at Garland Summer Musicals

Released 2026-07-17

Patty Granville, who founded Garland Summer Musicals in 1982, reprises her favorite role as Dolly Levi in Hello Dolly! for the fifth time July 17-26. Performances will be held in Granville Performing Arts Center July 17-26. For tickets or more information, visit garlandsummermusicals.org or call 972
