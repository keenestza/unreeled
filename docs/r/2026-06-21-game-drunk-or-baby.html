Drunk or Baby

Released 2026-06-21

A party game for 2–8 players where you decide: is it a drunk person… or a baby? Players read ridiculous scenarios and must guess whether they describe someone who's drunk or an actual baby. Designed for laughs, surprises, and unforgettable moments with friends.
